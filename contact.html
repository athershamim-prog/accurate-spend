Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the demo cover?
We tailor every demo to the client. Typically we cover: how the Accurate Spend reporting process works end-to-end, how we handle your specific data formats, what the consultant engagement model looks like, timeline to first submission, and pricing — followed by open Q&A.
How quickly can we get started?
Onboarding is fast — typically days, not months. Unlike enterprise software implementations, we don't require lengthy system setups. Once we understand your data and reporting scope, we can begin the process very quickly.
What data formats can you accept?
Any format your team currently uses. This includes Excel spreadsheets, CSV files, SAP or Oracle exports, Salesforce reports, Veeva extracts, or any custom data file. We don't require you to reformat or restructure your data — our team handles all transformation.
How does pricing work?
We provide transparent, straightforward pricing based on your reporting scope — primarily the volume of records and complexity of your data sources. We'll walk through pricing on the demo call. What we guarantee: it will be significantly less than what you'd pay with MediSpend, Alanda, or comparable enterprise platforms.
Do you only handle federal Open Payments reporting?
Yes — Accurate Spend is exclusively focused on federal CMS Open Payments reporting under the Sunshine Act. This focus allows us to provide deeper expertise, tighter processes, and better pricing than generalist platforms. We do not provide state transparency reporting.
We're already using another vendor — can we switch?
Absolutely. Many of our clients have transitioned from other vendors — typically because they wanted more consultant support, more flexibility with their data, or simply to reduce costs. We manage the transition smoothly, including importing historical data and maintaining continuity of your compliance record.
