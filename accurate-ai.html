Background

What Is Accurate AI?

Most compliance platforms are built to handle the most common scenarios — but every pharmaceutical organization has unique data structures, workflows, and regulatory obligations that standard software simply wasn't designed to accommodate.

Accurate AI is our artificial intelligence engine, built specifically to solve compliance problems for pharma and life sciences companies. It's the intelligence layer that powers Accurate Spend — reading source documents, mapping data to regulatory categories, and catching errors before they become submission problems. Every model is informed by over a decade of hands-on pharma compliance expertise.

Beyond powering Accurate Spend, we also put Accurate AI to work on bespoke problems — a custom HCP payment tracking portal, a data pipeline connecting your ERP to a regulatory filing, or a custom approval workflow — scoped and built with compliance accuracy as the foundation.

Built on Domain Expertise

Unlike general-purpose AI tools, Accurate AI reflects deep knowledge of the Sunshine Act, state reporting rules, HCP engagement compliance, and the data realities of pharma commercial operations.
