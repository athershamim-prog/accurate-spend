Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the HCP Engage demo cover?
We tailor every demo. Typically we walk an engagement end-to-end — onboarding with NPI verification and OIG/SAM debarment screening, fair-market-value enforcement, approval workflows, proof-of-performance capture, and the audit-ready record — followed by open Q&A.
Which engagement types does it handle?
Advisory boards, speaker programs, investigator/research agreements, meals and transfers of value, and training — each governed with the appropriate approvals, documentation, and FMV controls.
How does it relate to Accurate Spend federal reporting?
HCP Engage governs the interactions before payment; Accurate Spend handles the federal CMS Open Payments reporting of those transfers of value. They complement each other, and can be discussed together on your call.
How quickly can we get started?
After the demo we'll scope your engagement types and approval flows, then move quickly to configure a pilot — no lengthy enterprise implementation required.
